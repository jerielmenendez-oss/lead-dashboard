1980s

2000s

Today

Meet the Master

Anthony Jones

With over five decades behind the chair, Anthony Jones has perfected his craft through thousands of clients and countless styles. What began as a young man's passion in 1967 has become a Gulf Coast institution.

Anthony's philosophy is simple: every client deserves to leave feeling like the best version of themselves. That personal touch and dedication to excellence is why families have trusted him for three generations.
