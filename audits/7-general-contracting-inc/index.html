Top 3 Things to Fix First

1. Build a Professional Website

Highest Priority

A clean, mobile-friendly website is the single biggest thing you can do to grow your business. It should include: your services (general contracting, remodeling, new construction), your service area across Baldwin County, photos of completed projects, your BBB A+ badge, a contact form, and your phone number on every page. This is how you turn 17 years of experience into a 24/7 lead generation machine.

2. Claim & Optimize Your Google Business Profile

Highest Priority

Go to business.google.com and claim your listing. Add your services, hours, service area, photos, and a description highlighting your 475 projects and A+ BBB rating. A verified Google Business Profile is free and is the #1 way to appear in Google Maps when someone searches “contractor near me.” Start asking satisfied customers to leave Google reviews — even 10-15 reviews will put you ahead of many competitors.

3. Create a Facebook Page with Project Photos

Medium Priority

A business Facebook page is free and takes 15 minutes to set up. Post before-and-after photos of your best projects. In construction, people hire with their eyes — they want to see what you’ve built. Even posting one project a week will build a portfolio that sells for you. Tag your location in every post so locals see your work in their feed.
