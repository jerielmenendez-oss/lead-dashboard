Executive Summary

Beachfront Construction LLC has 25+ years of experience specializing in water damage restoration and custom carpentry in Gulf Shores. You offer emergency services — the kind of work where homeowners need to find a contractor immediately.

But your website is a basic WordPress site using the free Hestia theme, which makes it look like a template rather than a professional business. The site loads slowly due to heavy inline CSS and JavaScript. You have zero Facebook reviews, providing no social proof for new customers. For emergency water damage work, speed and trust are everything — your website needs to load fast, display your phone number prominently, and immediately convey your 25 years of experience.

Key Findings

Free WordPress Theme Looks Generic

The Hestia theme is a free, widely-used WordPress template. Customers who have seen this theme on other sites will recognize it, which undermines your unique brand. A custom design that reflects your 25 years of experience would set you apart.

Slow Page Loading — Heavy Inline Code

Your site has excessive inline CSS and JavaScript, which slows down page loading. For emergency water damage calls, every second matters. A homeowner with a burst pipe will call the first plumber or contractor whose site loads — if yours is slow, they’ll move on.

No Online Scheduling for Emergency Services

You offer emergency water damage restoration, but there’s no way to request emergency service online. A prominent “Emergency? Call Now” button with your phone number, or an emergency request form, would convert more visitors into calls.

Zero Facebook Reviews

Your Facebook page has no reviews. After 25+ years of work, this is a massive untapped resource. Potential customers checking your Facebook and seeing zero reviews may assume the business is new or inactive.

25+ Years Experience — Powerful Credibility

A quarter century in construction, specializing in water damage restoration and custom carpentry, is an extraordinary track record. This should be the headline on your website — “Serving Gulf Shores for 25+ Years.” It instantly builds trust with homeowners in crisis.

Emergency Services — High-Intent Market

Water damage restoration is an emergency service — homeowners searching for it need help immediately and will pay a premium for fast response. This is one of the highest-value search categories for a contractor. A fast, professional website optimized for “water damage restoration Gulf Shores” would capture high-value emergency calls.

Top 3 Things to Fix First

1. Rebuild the Website for Speed & Emergency Conversions

Highest Priority

Replace the free theme with a professional, fast-loading design. Put your phone number and an “Emergency? Call Now” button at the top of every page. Optimize for mobile — most emergency searches happen on phones. Remove the heavy inline code that slows loading. For water damage restoration, your site needs to load in under 3 seconds.

2. Create Emergency-Focused Landing Pages

Highest Priority

Build dedicated pages targeting emergency keywords: “water damage restoration Gulf Shores,” “flood damage repair Orange Beach,” “emergency contractor Baldwin County.” These pages should be stripped down to essentials: what you do, how fast you respond, your phone number, and a call-to-action. This is where the high-value calls come from.

3. Collect Reviews — Especially for Emergency Work

Medium Priority

After 25 years, you have satisfied clients. Ask them for Google and Facebook reviews, especially clients you helped in an emergency. “They came out the same night my pipe burst” is the kind of review that wins you the next emergency call. Go from zero reviews to 15+ and your online reputation will transform.
