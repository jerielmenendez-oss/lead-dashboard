Blonde & Blush Beauty Studio is using a GlossGenius booking platform subdomain as its entire web presence. Detailed Findings

Website Quality (4/25)

No Custom Website — Using Booking Platform as "Website"

The business has no standalone website. The GlossGenius booking page at blondeandblushbeautystudio.glossgenius.com is being used as the primary web presence. It handles booking but fails completely as a website.

No Control Over Design or Content

GlossGenius controls the page layout, URL structure, meta tags, and available features. Cannot add a gallery page, blog posts, testimonials, Google reviews integration, or landing pages for promotions.

SEO Fundamentals (1/20)

Zero SEO Foundation

Title tags, meta descriptions, and headings are all platform-controlled. No schema markup, no custom pages, no keyword targeting possible. This business does not exist in organic Google search results.

Cannot Rank for Local Searches

Search queries like "beauty studio Robertsdale AL," "hair salon near me," or "blonde specialist Baldwin County" will never return the GlossGenius page. Google treats glossgenius.com as the domain authority, not the business.

Google Business Profile (7/20)

GBP Is the Only Local SEO Asset

Google Business Profile is active with listing visible on Maps. NAP: 18544 Hammond St, Robertsdale AL 36567. However, GBP website link points to the GlossGenius subdomain, and no local keyword targeting exists.

Social Media (9/15)

Active Facebook & Instagram

Both profiles are engaged and active with good content. The owner clearly has marketing instinct — this is the strongest asset. It just needs a proper website to anchor the entire online presence.

User Experience (5/10)

Booking Works Well

GlossGenius handles appointments effectively. Service list with pricing is visible. Mobile-friendly pages.

No Portfolio, Story, or Testimonials

The booking page shows what you can book and what it costs, but tells no story. No emotional connection, no portfolio, no reason to choose Blonde & Blush over competitors.

Online Reputation (4/10)

No Review Generation System

No visible system for driving Google reviews. Subdomain URLs hurt citation consistency across directories.

Prioritized Recommendations

1. Register a Custom Domain & Build a Website

High ImpactPriority: Immediate
What's Wrong
No standalone website exists. The GlossGenius subdomain provides zero SEO value and cannot rank in Google.
Why It Matters
The #1 way new customers find local beauty studios is through Google search. Without a rankable website, Blonde & Blush is invisible to everyone not already following on social media.
How to Fix
Register blondeandblushbeauty.com or similar. Build a custom website with About, Services, Gallery, and Contact pages. Embed or link GlossGenius booking on the new site — keep using it for scheduling.
Estimated Impact
Foundation for all SEO. Makes the business discoverable for dozens of relevant local search terms.

High ImpactPriority: Immediate
What's Wrong

3. Add BeautySalon + LocalBusiness Schema Markup

High ImpactPriority: This Month
What's Wrong
No structured data markup exists anywhere. No LocalBusiness or BeautySalon schema.
Why It Matters
Schema markup enables rich search results with hours, ratings, and services directly in Google.
How to Fix

Estimated Impact
Enhanced search appearance and 10-15% improvement in click-through rates.
