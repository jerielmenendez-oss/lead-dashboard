Executive Summary

Blonde & Blush Beauty Studio has strong brand energy and active social media, but is missing the most fundamental piece: a real website. Currently relying solely on a GlossGenius subdomain, the studio cannot rank in Google search results. GlossGenius is excellent for booking, but a subdomain on their platform will never compete with custom-domain websites. In Robertsdale, where a beauty studio could easily own local search, this is a significant missed opportunity. The active social media demonstrates marketing savvy — it just needs a proper website to funnel into.
