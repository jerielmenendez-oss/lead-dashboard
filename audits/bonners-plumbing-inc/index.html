What This Means for Your Business

You've been in business since 1985 — 41 years serving Baldwin County. That's a track record most plumbers would kill for. Technical SEO — 36/100
PASS Your website uses HTTPS — visitors see a secure padlock.
PASS Your site has a mobile viewport tag for phone viewing.
FAIL No structured data (schema markup) — Google can't automatically read your hours, services, or location.
Schema markup is code that tells Google exactly what your business does, where you are, and when you're open. Without it, Google has to guess — and competitors who have it rank higher.
FAIL No sitemap detected — Google may be missing some of your pages.
WARN Heavy page load — multiple font families, animation libraries, and large images slow things down.
Your site loads 4+ font families (Sora, Open Sans, Barlow, Roboto), a Lottie animation library, and a large hero image. On a phone with slow mobile data, this could take 5+ seconds to load. Google penalizes slow sites.
WARN Built on Thryv platform — limits your SEO customization options.
Thryv is a small-business platform, not a professional web platform. It restricts how much you can optimize for search engines.
On-Page SEO — 30/100
PASS Title tag includes your city and business name: "Plumbing Services | Foley, AL | Bonner's Plumbing Inc."
FAIL No meta description — Google is writing your search result description for you.
When people search for you, the text under your site name in Google is whatever Google picks from your page. A meta description lets you control that message — something like "Family-owned plumbing in Foley, AL since 1985. Licensed, bonded, insured. Call for a free estimate."
FAIL No H1 heading tag — your main headline uses an H3 tag instead.
"Efficient & Responsible Plumbing Company" should be an H1, not an H3. H1 tags tell Google what the page is about. Missing this is like having a book with no title on the cover.
FAIL Images have no alt text descriptions.
Your logo and service images have no descriptions for Google or visually impaired visitors. Every image should describe what it shows.
PASS Good internal navigation with clear service categories.
Plumbing Services, Water Heater Services, Gallery, Contact — clean and logical structure.

Fix These 3 Things First
1
Add LocalBusiness schema markup and meta descriptions to every page.
This is the highest-impact technical fix. Schema markup tells Google your hours, services, location, and service area automatically. Meta descriptions control what people see in search results. Together, these can improve your rankings without changing anything visible on the site.
2
Add a testimonials section with your best customer reviews.
After 41 years, you should have dozens of great reviews. Put 5-10 of your best on the homepage and create a dedicated testimonials page. This builds trust and helps Google see your business as reputable.
3
Add your physical address and a Google Maps embed to the contact page.
Google uses your address to verify you're a real local business. Without it, you're harder to find in "plumber near me" searches. This is a 5-minute fix that makes a real difference.
