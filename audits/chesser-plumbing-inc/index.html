What This Means for Your Business

Your website exists, and that puts you ahead of some competitors — but it's built on GoDaddy's basic website builder, which limits what Google can do with it. For a plumbing company that wants to be found by new customers searching online, there's a lot of room to improve here.

On-Page SEO — 25/100
FAIL No meta description — Google doesn't know how to describe your business in search results.
When someone searches "plumber near me," Google shows a short description under your website name. Without a meta description, Google picks random text from your page — and it usually looks bad.
FAIL Your images don't have descriptions (alt text) that tell Google what they show.
Every image on your site should have a short description. This helps Google understand your content and helps visually impaired customers too.
FAIL No dedicated pages for each service you offer.
You mention "repairs, installations, and maintenance" generically but don't have separate pages for each. A page about "Emergency Drain Repair" would rank for that specific search.
WARN Thin content — your homepage doesn't have enough text for Google to understand what you do.
Pages with more detailed, helpful content rank higher. Your site reads more like a brochure than a resource.
WARN Your "Recent Projects" page is completely empty.
Proof: chesserplumbinginc.com/recent-projects — no project photos, descriptions, or content.
Local SEO — 40/100

Content & Reputation — 30/100
FAIL No customer reviews or testimonials on your website.
88% of people trust online reviews as much as personal recommendations. Your site should showcase your best reviews — right now there's nothing.
WARN Only 6 reviews found online — you need more.
Your competitors in the Foley area have 20-100+ reviews. More reviews = higher Google rankings and more trust from new customers.
FAIL No blog or helpful content that would bring people to your site.
Articles like "5 Signs Your Water Heater Needs Replacing" bring in people searching for plumbing help — and they become customers.
WARN Your "About" section is minimal — it doesn't tell your story.
People hire plumbers they trust. Tell them about Steven, the team, how long you've been doing this, and why you're different.
Fix These 3 Things First
1
Add dedicated service pages with the cities you serve in the content.
Right now, Google has no reason to show your site when someone searches "plumber Foley AL" or "drain cleaning Gulf Shores." Each service needs its own page with real descriptions and location keywords.
2
Fill out your "Recent Projects" page with real photos and descriptions.
That empty page is hurting you. Before-and-after photos of plumbing jobs build trust and give Google more content to index. It's free marketing.
3
Add meta descriptions and image alt text to every page.
These are the basics that tell Google what your site is about. Without them, you're invisible to searches. This is a quick fix with a big impact.
