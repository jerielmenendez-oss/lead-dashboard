Website Quality (1/25)

Website Is a Free Google Placeholder (placeid.site)

The current "website" is a Google-generated placeholder at placeid.site. These pages often fail to load entirely, redirect unpredictably, and offer zero control over content, design, or SEO.

Zero Branding or Customization

The placeholder page has no logo, no photos, no service menu, no pricing, and no personality. It's a blank shell with an address. A Latin barbershop with character and culture should showcase that identity.

SEO Fundamentals (0/20)

Zero SEO Foundation

No title tag, no meta description, no heading structure, no schema markup, no sitemap. Google does not index placeid.site pages in organic search. Eduardo's is invisible in organic search results.

Not Indexable by Google

The placeid.site pages are not indexed by Google. The only discovery path is Google Maps/GBP. Anyone searching "barbershop Foley AL" will never find Eduardo's through his website.

No Website to Support GBP

Without a real website, Eduardo's can't build local authority, earn backlinks, or rank for "barbershop near me" in organic results. Local keywords are completely missing.

Social Media (5/15)

User Experience (1/10)
