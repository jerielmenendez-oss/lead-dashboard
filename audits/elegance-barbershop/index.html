Executive Summary

Elegance Barbershop's online presence is fragmented across three separate platforms: a Hub-24 template site with no custom domain, a separate WordPress blog, and Instagram. This fragmentation dilutes SEO authority, confuses customers, and makes it nearly impossible to rank in Google. Instead of one strong website collecting all search equity, link juice is split three ways — and none of the platforms are optimized. For a Latin barbershop with active social engagement, consolidating into a single professional website would dramatically improve visibility.
