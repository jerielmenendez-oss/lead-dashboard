Prioritized Recommendations

1

Fix H1 Tag Structure

HIGH
What's WrongMultiple H1 tags confuse search engines about the page topic.
Why It MattersHeading hierarchy is a fundamental SEO signal. Multiple H1s dilute relevance.
How to FixKeep one H1 (e.g., "24/7 Gym in Gulf Shores, AL") and change others to H2/H3.
Estimated ImpactImproved primary keyword rankings. Quick fix with measurable benefit.
2

Add Visible Membership Pricing

HIGH
What's WrongNo pricing visible on the website.
Why It Matters76% of gym seekers want pricing before visiting. Hidden pricing increases bounce rate.
How to FixAdd pricing page with membership tiers, day passes, and specials.
Estimated ImpactReduces bounce rate and increases qualified leads.
3

Integrate Class Schedule

MEDIUM
What's WrongNo schedule or availability info online.
Why It MattersMembers want to plan visits. Schedules increase engagement and return visits.
How to FixEmbed Google Calendar or create a weekly schedule page.
Estimated ImpactIncreases site utility and repeat visits.
4

Add LocalBusiness Schema

MEDIUM
What's WrongNo structured data on the site.
Why It MattersSchema enables rich search results with hours, ratings, price range.
How to FixAdd HealthClub schema via Wix SEO settings.
Estimated ImpactRich snippets could increase CTR by 20-30%.
5

Target Tourist Keywords

MEDIUM
What's WrongNo content targeting tourists seeking day passes.
Why It MattersGulf Shores is a major tourist destination with high-intent gym searchers.
How to FixCreate content for "gym near Gulf Shores beach", "day pass gym" queries.
Estimated ImpactCaptures seasonal tourist traffic and day pass revenue.
