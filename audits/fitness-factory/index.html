Executive Summary

Fitness Factory sits in one of the most valuable local markets — a 24/7 gym in Gulf Shores, a tourist destination — yet its website is a generic Wix template failing to capitalize on this advantage. Under new ownership since 2022, the gym has opportunity for a fresh start, but the current site with its intrusive chat widget, template design, and thin content is not converting the massive seasonal traffic Gulf Shores attracts. Tourists searching for 'gym Gulf Shores' should be finding Fitness Factory, but the website is not built for that.
