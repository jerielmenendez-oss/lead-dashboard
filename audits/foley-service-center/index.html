Detailed Findings

Website Quality (4/25)

Free GoDaddy Builder Site — No Custom Domain

The website is built on GoDaddy's free site builder. The URL (site-i7ew1qx7q.godaddysites.com) is impossible to remember, share, or put on a business card. A GoDaddy VPS hosting ad banner sits at the top of every page.

GoDaddy Branding Undermines Professionalism

The footer reads "Powered by GoDaddy," making the business look amateur. Trust is everything in auto service — this site erodes it before a customer even reads the content.

SEO Fundamentals (2/20)

Duplicate H1 Headings (10+ Times)

The page has duplicate H1 headings repeated more than 10 times. This is a serious SEO error that confuses search engines about what the page is about.

Template Default Meta Data

Title tags and meta descriptions are GoDaddy template defaults, not optimized for local search. The godaddysites.com subdomain also gets deprioritized in search results.

Google Business Profile (5/20)

GBP Exists but Weakened

Google Business Profile exists but links to a godaddysites.com URL. NAP consistency across directories is uncertain.

Gmail Address Instead of Professional Email

The Gmail address (FOLEYSERVICECENTER@GMAIL.COM) undermines professionalism. Professional email on a custom domain is a baseline trust signal for both customers and Google.

Social Media (1/15)

Broken Facebook Link — No Other Social

The Facebook link on the website redirects to facebook.com/login instead of the business page. No Instagram or other social media presence. A broken link is worse than no link at all.

User Experience (3/10)

Contact Form Works

The contact form with reCAPTCHA works, which is a positive. However, content is thin with no testimonials and no detailed service descriptions.

Outdated Copyright & Thin Content

Copyright year shows 2025. No testimonials, no service-specific pages, and no blog. Thin content cannot rank for anything meaningful.

Online Reputation (2/10)

No Reviews or Testimonials on Site

No customer testimonials on the website and no system to generate or display Google reviews. Without social proof, potential customers have no reason to trust the business online.
