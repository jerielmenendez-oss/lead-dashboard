01. Executive Summary

Foley Service Center's website is a free GoDaddy builder page that actively damages the business's credibility. The URL is an unmemorable GoDaddy subdomain (site-i7ew1qx7q.godaddysites.com), GoDaddy displays its own advertisements on the page, the Facebook link is broken, the contact email is a Gmail address instead of a business domain, and there are duplicate H1 heading tags causing SEO issues. While having a site is better than nothing, this one needs to be replaced — it signals "unprofessional" to every visitor.

02. Detailed Findings

Website Performance

Free GoDaddy Builder — Ads on Business Site

The site is built on GoDaddy's free tier, which means GoDaddy displays its own promotional ads on the page. This makes the business look unprofessional and sends visitors to GoDaddy instead of keeping them focused on your services.

Critical
No Custom Domain

The URL is "site-i7ew1qx7q.godaddysites.com" — a random string that is impossible to remember, share verbally, or put on a business card. A proper domain like "foleyservicecenter.com" would cost $12/year and dramatically improve credibility.

12 / 15

SEO & Technical Issues

Duplicate H1 Tags

The page has multiple H1 heading tags, which confuses search engines about the page's primary topic. Each page should have exactly one H1 tag. This is a basic SEO error that hurts search rankings.

8 / 10
Limited SEO Customization

GoDaddy's free builder offers minimal SEO control — limited meta tag customization, no structured data, no ability to create service-specific landing pages. The site cannot compete in local search results.

10 / 15

Online Presence & Credibility

Broken Facebook Link

The Facebook link on the website is broken and leads nowhere. This creates a dead end for visitors trying to learn more about the business, and broken links hurt SEO.

6 / 10
Gmail Email Address

The business uses a Gmail address for contact instead of a professional business email (e.g., info@foleyservicecenter.com). This reduces perceived professionalism and trustworthiness.

5 / 10

Content Quality

Thin Content — No Service Detail Pages

The site has minimal content with no dedicated pages for specific services (oil changes, brake repair, diagnostics, etc.). This means zero ranking potential for specific service queries.

8 / 10

03. Prioritized Recommendations

  1. Register a custom domain immediately — Secure "foleyservicecenter.com" or similar. This costs ~$12/year and instantly makes the business look professional. Stop using a GoDaddy subdomain.
  2. Build a proper professional website — Replace the free GoDaddy builder with a modern, custom site. Include service pages, real photos, contact form, and clear calls to action. Remove all third-party ads.
  3. Set up professional email — Use info@foleyservicecenter.com instead of Gmail. Professional email builds trust and is inexpensive to set up.
  4. Fix or rebuild Facebook presence — Repair the broken Facebook link and ensure the page is active with regular posts, customer interactions, and service updates.
  5. Fix SEO technical errors — Resolve duplicate H1 tags, add proper meta descriptions, implement schema markup for auto repair businesses.
  6. Create service-specific pages — Dedicated pages for each service (brakes, oil change, engine diagnostics, A/C repair, etc.) to capture specific search queries.
  7. Optimize Google Business Profile — Professional photos, complete services, regular posts, and link to the new professional website.
  8. Implement a review generation system — Actively collect Google reviews from satisfied customers. Respond to all reviews.
