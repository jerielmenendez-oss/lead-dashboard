Executive Summary

Green Sea Construction specializes in fences, gates, decks, railing, and bathroom remodeling in the Elberta area — close to Foley and the Baldwin County growth corridor. You have something many contractors don’t: positive customer reviews praising the quality of your shower and bathroom remodel work.

The problem is that those reviews are sitting on Yelp where most homeowners will never see them. You have no website, no way to showcase before-and-after photos, and no way for someone searching “deck builder near Foley” or “bathroom remodel Elberta” to find you. A simple website with a project gallery would turn those happy customers into a lead-generating machine.

Key Findings

No Website At All

Green Sea Construction has no website. For a business that builds fences, decks, and does bathroom remodels — all highly visual work — this is a huge missed opportunity. Homeowners want to see what you’ve built before they call. Without a website, they can’t.

No Google Business Profile

Without a Google Business Profile, you don’t appear on Google Maps when someone searches for contractors in Elberta or nearby Foley. The map results at the top of Google get the most clicks — and you’re not in them.

No Online Portfolio or Before-and-After Photos

Fence, deck, and bathroom remodel work is sold visually. Homeowners want to see completed projects before they hire. Without a website or social media gallery, your best work is invisible to potential customers.

Positive Reviews & 141 Yelp Photos

Your Yelp listing has 141 photos and positive reviews praising your shower remodel work. That’s an incredible amount of visual proof — far more than most competitors. You also have a Facebook page and a BBB A rating. These are assets that should be on a website. Right now they’re scattered across platforms where most homeowners won’t find them.

Elberta Location — Growing Market

Elberta is close to Foley and the rapidly growing Baldwin County corridor. As new homes go up and existing homes need updates, the demand for fences, decks, and remodels is only increasing. Being near this growth zone with a website would capture that demand.

Top 3 Things to Fix First

1. Build a Website with a Project Gallery

Highest Priority

Create a mobile-friendly website with separate pages for each service (fences, decks, railings, bathroom remodels) and fill each page with before-and-after project photos. Include your Yelp reviews, your phone number on every page, and a contact form. For visual trades like yours, a gallery page is worth more than any amount of text.

2. Claim & Optimize Google Business Profile

Highest Priority

Go to business.google.com and create your listing. Set your service area to include Elberta, Foley, Gulf Shores, Orange Beach, and surrounding communities. Upload your best project photos and ask your satisfied customers to leave Google reviews. This is free and puts you on the map — literally.

3. Start an Instagram Account for Project Photos

Medium Priority

Instagram is perfect for contractors who do visual work. Post a before-and-after photo of every fence, deck, and bathroom you complete. Tag your location on every post. Homeowners in the area will start discovering your work in their feeds. This is free marketing that builds your portfolio over time.
