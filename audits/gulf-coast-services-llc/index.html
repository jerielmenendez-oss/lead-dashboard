Top 3 Things to Fix First

1. Build a Website Targeting Stucco Keywords

Highest Priority

Create a website optimized for stucco-specific searches: “stucco contractor Foley AL,” “stucco repair Baldwin County,” “stucco installation Gulf Shores,” “EIFS contractor Alabama.” Include photos of completed stucco work, your service area, and your phone number on every page. 2. Create & Optimize Google Business Profile

Highest Priority

Go to business.google.com and create your listing. Select “Stucco Contractor” as your primary category. Add your service area, photos, hours, and phone number. Your 4.4-star reviews will show up right in the search results, immediately building trust. Then ask more past clients to leave reviews.

3. Set Up a Professional Email

Medium Priority

Create a professional email like info@gulfcoastservicesllc.com. General contractors who hire stucco subs communicate primarily through email for bids and scheduling. A professional email builds credibility and makes it easy for GCs to add you to their vendor list.
