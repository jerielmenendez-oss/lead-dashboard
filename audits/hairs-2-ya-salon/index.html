Detailed Findings

Website Quality (0/25)

No Website Exists

No website exists. The only online touchpoints are Facebook and GBP. When someone searches "hair salon Foley AL," Google prioritizes businesses with websites. Without one, Hairs 2 Ya is buried beneath competitors. Facebook pages do not rank well for local search terms.

SEO Fundamentals (0/20)

No SEO Footprint

No website means no SEO footprint. The salon cannot rank organically for any keyword. Search terms like "hair salon Foley AL," "women's haircut near me," and "salon on McKenzie St Foley" have purchase-ready intent. Every one of those searches goes to a competitor.

Content Quality

Content Trapped on Third-Party Platforms

All content lives on platforms the salon does not own. Facebook can change algorithms or restrict reach at any time. Owned content on a website is the only reliable way to control your brand message and attract search traffic. Facebook reach for business pages averages 2-5% of followers.

Google Business Profile (6/20)

GBP Exists but Under-Optimized

GBP exists but is likely under-optimized without a website to link to. Limited directory citations reduce local ranking signals. Google's local pack weighs website links, citation volume, and review count heavily.

Social Media (4/15)

Facebook Only — No Instagram

Facebook is the only social channel. No Instagram presence, which is the primary platform for salon discovery. No online booking system. Instagram is the number one platform where people discover salons and stylists through visual content.

User Experience (0/10)

No Online Booking or Digital Experience

No website and no online booking system means clients must call during business hours — a friction point that costs appointments. The client who wants to schedule at 9pm from their couch goes to a competitor instead.

Online Reputation (3/10)

Limited Review Presence

Reviews exist only on GBP. No testimonials on a website, no social proof beyond basic Google reviews. No email marketing or client retention systems in place.

High ImpactPriority: Immediate
What's Wrong
