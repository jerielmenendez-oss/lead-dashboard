03. Prioritized Recommendations

  1. Build a professional website with online booking — A modern, mobile-first site showcasing services, pricing, stylist profiles, a gallery of work, and integrated appointment booking. This is the most impactful change possible.
  2. Launch and grow an Instagram profile — Hair salons thrive on visual platforms. Post before/after transformations, styling tips, and behind-the-scenes content. Instagram is where potential clients discover new salons.
  3. Fully optimize Google Business Profile — Professional photos, complete service list, regular posts, Q&A responses, and link to the new website. This directly impacts local search ranking.
  4. Implement local SEO targeting — Optimize for "hair salon Foley AL," "salon near me," "women's haircut Foley," and similar terms. Build citations across 30+ directories.
  5. Set up a review generation system — Encourage happy clients to leave Google reviews after every appointment. Respond to all reviews within 24 hours.
  6. Create service-specific landing pages — Dedicated pages for cuts, color, styling, treatments — each optimized for search to capture specific service queries.
