Executive Summary

House of Hair Salon & Beauty Bar has significant SEO challenges that are likely costing the business substantial search visibility in the Daphne market. The homepage is missing both an H1 tag and meta description — two of the most fundamental SEO elements. The site content is extremely thin, consisting mostly of call-to-action buttons with minimal descriptive text for Google to index. Additionally, the .rocks domain extension is unconventional and may carry less inherent trust than a standard .com. With targeted improvements to on-page content and technical SEO, this site has strong upside potential.

Prioritized Recommendations

1. Add H1 Tag and Meta Description High Impact

What's wrong: The homepage has neither an H1 tag nor a meta description — the two most important on-page SEO elements are completely missing.
Why it matters: Without these, Google has almost no guidance on what this page is about or what to show searchers in results.
How to fix: Add an H1 like "House of Hair Salon & Beauty Bar — Daphne, Alabama" and a meta description highlighting services, location, and unique value.
Estimated impact: This single change could dramatically improve visibility for "hair salon Daphne AL" searches.

2. Add Substantial Service Content to Homepage High Impact

What's wrong: The homepage is almost entirely buttons and CTAs with barely any descriptive content.
Why it matters: Google needs text content to understand and rank pages. With minimal content, there's nothing to rank for.
How to fix: Add sections describing services (cuts, color, styling, beauty bar treatments), include pricing ranges, stylist bios, and salon features.
Estimated impact: Could open up dozens of keyword ranking opportunities currently being missed entirely.

3. Add LocalBusiness Schema Markup Medium Impact

What's wrong: Only generic WebPage/Organization schema exists — no local business structured data.
Why it matters: LocalBusiness (specifically BeautySalon) schema enables rich Google results with hours, reviews, phone number, and map pin.
How to fix: Add BeautySalon schema with NAP data, hours, geo-coordinates, services offered, and price range.
Estimated impact: Rich results increase click-through rates by 20-30%.

4. Consider Acquiring a .com Domain Medium Impact

What's wrong: The .rocks TLD is uncommon and may seem less legitimate to potential customers.
Why it matters: Users inherently trust .com domains more. An unusual TLD can reduce click-through rates and perceived credibility.
How to fix: Check availability of houseofhairsalon.com or houseofhairdaphne.com. If acquired, 301 redirect the .rocks domain.
Estimated impact: Incremental trust improvement — particularly for new customers finding you through search.

5. Add Before/After Gallery Moderate Impact

What's wrong: No visual portfolio showcasing the salon's work.
Why it matters: Before/after photos are the #1 conversion tool for salons and generate highly shareable content for social media and Google Images.
How to fix: Create a gallery page with optimized images, alt text including service names and location, and proper image schema.
Estimated impact: Drives Google Image traffic and significantly increases conversion rate for site visitors.
