Top 3 Things to Fix First

1. Build a Professional Website

Highest Priority

Your website should feature your services, your license credentials (#26824), your BuildZoom top-13% ranking, photos of your best projects, your BBB A+ badge, and a contact form. Every page should have your phone number. This is the difference between being found by homeowners searching Google and being invisible.

2. Claim Your Google Business Profile

Highest Priority

Go to business.google.com and create your listing. Add your service area (Loxley, Foley, Daphne, Fairhope, and surrounding Baldwin County), upload project photos, list your services, and include your license number. This is free and is the fastest way to appear in Google Maps when someone searches “contractor near me.”

3. Start Collecting Google Reviews

Highest Priority

After 51 projects, you have satisfied customers who would leave a review if you asked. Create a simple Google review link (your GBP provides one) and send it to past clients via text. Even 10–15 five-star reviews would put you ahead of most competitors in the area. Reviews are the #1 factor in a homeowner’s decision to call.
