Critical Findings

Wasted Premium Domain

kuttinup.vip Shows Only a Square Checkout Page

Visiting your website shows a Square checkout cart with payment icons (CashApp, Apple Pay, Visa, etc.) and an Unsplash stock photo. There are no services, no hours, no contact info, no photos of your work — nothing. Your premium .vip domain communicates exclusivity, but the page communicates abandonment.

Stock Photo

Generic Image Instead of Your Work

The only image on your site is a stock photo from Unsplash — not your actual shop, not your barbers, not your styles. Customers want to see the real business. A stock photo signals the site was never finished.

No Instagram

Missing the #1 Platform for Beauty & Barber

No Instagram account exists. In the beauty and barbering industry, Instagram is where customers discover new shops through before/after photos and transformation videos. Without it, you're invisible to an entire generation of potential clients.

What's Working

Modern Payment Processing

You accept CashApp, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Visa, Mastercard, AmEx, Discover, and JCB. Your payment infrastructure is modern — now your web presence needs to match it.

Recommendations

1. Build a Real Website on kuttinup.vip

High PriorityWeek 1-2
What:
Build a proper website on your existing .vip domain. Include services (beauty and barber), team profiles, gallery, hours, location map, contact info, and booking.
Why:
You already own a premium domain. Right now it's wasted on a checkout page. A real website turns that domain into a 24/7 sales tool that works while you sleep.

2. Create an Instagram Business Profile

High PriorityWeek 1
What:
Set up @kuttinup or @kuttinupvip on Instagram. Post 3-5 photos per week of fresh cuts, styles, and beauty services. Use hashtags: #PascagoulaBarber, #MississippiHair, #GulfCoastBarber.
Why:
Before/after content is the #1 driver of new beauty and barber clients. One well-shot transformation photo can bring in multiple bookings.

3. Replace Stock Photo with Real Images

High PriorityWeek 1
What:
Take photos of the actual shop interior, team members, and finished work (haircuts, styles, beauty services). Replace the Unsplash stock image immediately.
Why:
Even phone-quality photos of real work are infinitely better than a stock image. Customers want to see what they'll actually experience.

4. Set Up Online Booking

Medium PriorityMonth 1
What:
Implement Square Appointments (you already use Square for payments), Booksy, or Vagaro for direct appointment booking.
Why:
You have the payment infrastructure. Adding booking lets customers schedule anytime — evenings, weekends, whenever they're thinking about getting a cut.

5. Optimize Google Business Profile

Medium PriorityMonth 1
What:
Update GBP with your real website link, professional photos, complete service list, business hours, and a compelling description of your beauty + barber concept.
Why:
Right now your GBP website link sends customers to a checkout page. Fixing this immediately improves the experience for everyone who finds you on Google.
