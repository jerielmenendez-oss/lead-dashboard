Prioritized Recommendations

1. Add a Compelling Meta Description

High ImpactPriority: Immediate
What's Wrong
No meta description exists, letting Google auto-generate often irrelevant snippets.
Why It Matters
A strong meta description with keywords like "salon Orange Beach AL" can increase clicks by 15-25%.
How to Fix
In Squarespace SEO settings, add: "Lucy Jane's Classic Beauty & Barber — full-service salon and barbershop in Orange Beach, AL. Haircuts, color, styling & grooming. Book online today!"
Estimated Impact
15-25% increase in organic click-through rate within 2-4 weeks.

2. Change Homepage H1 to Business-Relevant Heading

High ImpactPriority: Immediate
What's Wrong
The H1 tag reads "About Us" — a generic label with zero SEO value.
Why It Matters
The H1 is a top-3 on-page ranking factor. Google uses it to understand your page's primary topic.
How to Fix
Change to something like: "Lucy Jane's Classic Beauty & Barber — Orange Beach's Premier Salon & Barbershop"
Estimated Impact
Improved rankings for local salon/barbershop keywords within 4-6 weeks.

3. Add Detailed Service Pages

High ImpactPriority: This Week
What's Wrong
No detailed service descriptions or pricing are visible on the website.
Why It Matters
Service pages create opportunities to rank for specific searches like "balayage Orange Beach" or "men's haircut Orange Beach." They also help potential clients make decisions.
How to Fix
Create individual service pages or a comprehensive services page with descriptions, approximate pricing, and duration for each service.
Estimated Impact
New ranking opportunities for long-tail service keywords. Increased conversion rate from informed visitors.

4. Add Team/Stylist Profiles

Medium ImpactPriority: This Month
What's Wrong
No team bios or stylist profiles are available on the site.
Why It Matters
Stylist profiles build personal connection and trust. They also add keyword-rich content and can rank for "stylists near me" searches.
How to Fix
Add a team page with photos, specialties, experience, and a personal touch for each stylist/barber.
Estimated Impact
Increased trust, engagement, and content depth for SEO.

5. Add LocalBusiness Schema Markup

Medium ImpactPriority: This Month
What's Wrong
No structured data helps Google understand the business details.
Why It Matters
Schema markup enables rich search results showing hours, ratings, price range, and more directly in Google.
How to Fix
Add JSON-LD BeautySalon schema via Squarespace's code injection feature in the header, including name, address, phone, hours, and services.
Estimated Impact
Enhanced search result appearance and potential 10-15% CTR improvement.
