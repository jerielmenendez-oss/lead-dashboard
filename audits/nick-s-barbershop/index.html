Critical Findings

No Owned Website

Business Has No Website

Your only web presence is a goto-where.com directory listing — a third-party platform you don't own, can't customize, and can't brand. If goto-where.com removes the listing, your entire online presence disappears.

No Social Media

Zero Social Presence

No Facebook. No Instagram. No TikTok. For a business with 237 five-star-quality reviews, this is leaving massive growth on the table. Your happy customers can't tag you or share their experience online.

No Direct Booking

Booking Only Via Third-Party

Online booking is only available through the goto-where.com directory. After-hours browsers have no way to schedule. Every competitor with direct booking is capturing customers you should have.

What's Working

237 Reviews & 4.8 Stars

Your Google reviews are your strongest asset. Customers love the experience — mentioning barbers Doug and Michelle by name, praising the welcoming atmosphere. A proper website and social presence would amplify this reputation massively.

Recommendations

1. Build a Custom Website

High PriorityWeek 1-2
What:
Register a domain and build a professional website with barber profiles, service menu, gallery, hours, and direct online booking.
Why:
You need to own your digital presence. A website you control works 24/7 — not just during business hours.

2. Create Instagram & Facebook Profiles

High PriorityWeek 1
What:
Set up business profiles on Instagram and Facebook. Post 2-3 photos per week of fresh cuts.
Why:
Barber transformation content drives massive organic reach. Your 237 satisfied customers are a built-in audience.

3. Set Up Direct Online Booking

High PriorityWeek 1-2
What:
Implement Square Appointments, Booksy, or Vagaro for direct booking.
Why:
After-hours browsers can't book with you right now. Direct booking captures those customers.

4. Optimize Google Business Profile

Medium PriorityMonth 1
What:
Update GBP with website link, booking link, professional photos, and complete service list.
Why:
Your GBP drives significant traffic. Optimizing it converts more of that traffic into appointments.

5. Start Video Content

Month 2-3Growth
What:
Instagram Reels and TikTok showing barber transformations.
Why:
Barber transformation videos are some of the highest-performing content on social media.
