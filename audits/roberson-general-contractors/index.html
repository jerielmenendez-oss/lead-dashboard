Top 3 Things to Fix First

1. Replace the Broken Website Immediately

Urgent

Your website has been down and is actively hurting your business right now. Every day it shows a 403 error is a day potential clients assume you’re out of business. You need a new, professional website on your existing domain (robersongeneralcontractors.com) with HTTPS, your portfolio of custom homes, services, service area, and a contact form. This is the single most urgent fix.

2. Claim & Optimize Google Business Profile

Highest Priority

Create or claim your Google Business Profile at business.google.com. List your service area across Gulf Shores, Orange Beach, Foley, and Baldwin County. Add photos of completed custom homes, your license info, and a description highlighting your 20+ year track record. This is free and will put you on Google Maps immediately.

3. Create Social Media & Start Collecting Reviews

Medium Priority

Set up a Facebook business page and an Instagram account. Post photos of your best custom home projects — the builds, the details, the finished product. Then start asking past clients for Google reviews. Going from zero reviews to even 10–15 would dramatically change how homeowners perceive you when they find you online.
