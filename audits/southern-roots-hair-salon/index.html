03. Prioritized Recommendations

  1. Build a professional, mobile-first website — Include service menu with pricing, stylist profiles, a portfolio/gallery page, online booking integration, and contact information. This is the single most impactful improvement.
  2. Create and grow an Instagram presence — Post before/after transformations, styling tips, product recommendations, and salon culture content. Use local hashtags like #FoleyAL #BaldwinCountyHair.
  3. Optimize Google Business Profile completely — Professional photos (interior, exterior, work samples), full service list, weekly Google Posts, and link to the new website.
  4. Target local search keywords — Optimize for "hair salon Foley AL," "Southern Roots Salon," "best salon Foley," "hair color Foley AL," and related terms.
  5. Implement online booking — Integrate a booking system (Square Appointments, Vagaro, or similar) into the website to convert visitors into booked clients 24/7.
  6. Build a review strategy — Systematically request reviews from satisfied clients. Aim for 50+ Google reviews within 6 months. Respond to every review personally.
