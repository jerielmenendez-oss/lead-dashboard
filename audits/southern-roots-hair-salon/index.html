Detailed Findings

Website Quality (0/25)

No Website Exists

No website exists. Online presence is limited to Facebook and GBP. Walk-in friendly salons benefit enormously from "near me" searches — these are people ready to visit right now. Without a website, Southern Roots does not appear in organic results for these searches and loses walk-in traffic to competitors who do.

SEO Fundamentals (0/20)

Zero Organic Search Footprint

No indexable content beyond GBP. Keywords like "walk-in hair salon Foley," "hair salon near me," and "Southern Roots Hair Salon" cannot lead to a website that does not exist. The branded search term is especially valuable and currently leads nowhere.

Content Quality

Content Trapped on Third-Party Platforms

Content is trapped on third-party platforms with no owned backup. Facebook's organic reach for business pages is 2-5%. Content posted there reaches a fraction of followers. A website ensures content is always accessible to anyone who searches.

Google Business Profile (6/20)

GBP Is the Only Verified Listing

GBP is the only verified local listing. Citation footprint is thin. Local pack rankings depend on citation volume, consistency, and a linked website. Southern Roots is at a disadvantage against salons with stronger citation profiles.

Social Media (4/15)

Facebook Only — No Instagram

Single social media channel (Facebook). No Instagram. No online booking. Hair salons thrive on visual platforms. Instagram is the top discovery channel for salons. No online booking means clients must call during business hours — a friction point that costs appointments.

User Experience (0/10)

No Digital Experience Available

Without a website, there is no digital user experience. No online booking system exists, meaning even walk-in clients cannot check wait times or availability before visiting.

Online Reputation (3/10)

Limited Review Presence

Reviews exist only on GBP. No testimonials featured on an owned website. No email marketing or client retention systems.
