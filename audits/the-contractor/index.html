Executive Summary

The Contractor specializes in ADU (Accessory Dwelling Unit) and guest house construction in Foley — a niche that’s booming as homeowners look for ways to add rental income, house aging parents, or create home offices.

Your website at thecontractors.llc is a GoDaddy Website Builder template that still has placeholder content and a test email (filler@godaddy.com) visible in the code. The copy repeats itself across pages. The .llc domain extension is unusual and harder for customers to type or remember — most people expect .com. You have an online booking link, which is good, but the site overall looks unfinished and doesn’t convey the professionalism that ADU construction demands.

Key Findings

GoDaddy Placeholder Content Still Visible

Your website still contains GoDaddy’s default test email (filler@godaddy.com) in the code, and copy repeats across multiple pages. This makes the site look unfinished and signals to customers that you didn’t invest time in your web presence — which raises questions about attention to detail.

Found: filler@godaddy.com in site code

.llc Domain Is Hard to Remember

The .llc top-level domain is unusual. When you tell someone “thecontractors dot llc,” most will type “thecontractors.com” instead and land on a different site or an error page. A .com domain would be easier for customers to remember and type correctly.

Repeated Copy Across Pages

The same text appears on multiple pages of your site. Google penalizes duplicate content, and visitors who see the same text repeated will lose confidence in the professionalism of the business.

ADU/Guest House Niche — Huge Opportunity

ADU construction is one of the fastest-growing segments in residential building. Homeowners are searching for “ADU builder Foley,” “guest house contractor Baldwin County,” and “backyard cottage builder.” Very few local contractors are specifically targeting these keywords — a properly optimized website could dominate this niche.

Online Booking Link Exists

Having an online booking or scheduling link shows you understand the value of making it easy for customers to reach you. This is a good feature that should be prominently featured on a better-built website.

Top 3 Things to Fix First

1. Replace the GoDaddy Template with a Professional Site

Highest Priority

Build a new website focused on ADU and guest house construction. Remove all placeholder content. Create dedicated pages: “ADU Construction,” “Guest Houses,” “Backyard Cottages,” “Home Additions.” Include project photos, a clear explanation of the ADU process, pricing guidance, and your booking link. Consider moving to a .com domain for better memorability.

2. Target ADU-Specific Keywords

Highest Priority

Optimize every page for ADU and guest house keywords: “ADU builder Foley AL,” “guest house contractor Baldwin County,” “accessory dwelling unit Gulf Shores,” “backyard cottage builder Alabama.” This is a niche with low competition and high intent — homeowners searching these terms are ready to build. You could rank on page one quickly.

3. Claim Google Business Profile & Collect Reviews

Medium Priority

Create or optimize your Google Business Profile with ADU/guest house as your specialty. Upload photos of completed ADU projects. Start asking every client for a Google review. In a niche market, even 5–10 reviews focused on ADU construction would make you the clear #1 choice.
