Prioritized Recommendations

1

Remove Duplicate ueniweb.com Page

URGENT
What's WrongTwo websites (mensroomoba.com and ueniweb.com) create duplicate content.
Why It MattersGoogle penalizes duplicates. Two sites split authority so neither ranks well.
How to FixContact ueniweb to remove/noindex. Set up 301 redirect to mensroomoba.com.
Estimated ImpactEliminates penalty risk and consolidates SEO authority.
2

Add LocalBusiness Schema

HIGH
What's WrongNo structured data on the website.
Why It MattersSchema enables rich results with ratings, hours, location.
