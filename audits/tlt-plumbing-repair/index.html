Executive Summary

TLT Plumbing & Repair has been serving Robertsdale for over 15 years as a locally owned plumbing company. You have a Nextdoor reputation in the neighborhood, which shows your work speaks for itself.

But Nextdoor only reaches people in your immediate area who are already on the app. When someone in Robertsdale, Silverhill, or Loxley searches Google for “plumber near me” — which is how most people find a plumber in 2026 — you simply don’t exist. No website, no Google Business Profile, no Facebook, no Instagram. Your competitors who show up in those searches are getting the calls that should be going to you.

Key Findings

No Website At All

TLT Plumbing has no website. When someone has a plumbing emergency and Googles “plumber Robertsdale AL,” they find your competitors — not you. A website with your services, phone number, and service area would change that immediately.

No Google Business Profile

Without a Google Business Profile, you’re invisible on Google Maps. The “map pack” at the top of plumbing searches gets the most clicks. Your competitors are there; you’re not. This is the single fastest free fix available.

No Facebook or Instagram

No social media presence was found. While plumbing isn’t as visual as construction, Facebook is still where many homeowners ask for recommendations. Not having a page means you’re excluded from those conversations.

15+ Years Experience — Strong Trust Signal

A decade and a half of plumbing experience is a powerful credential. Homeowners want a plumber who has seen it all. “Serving Robertsdale for 15+ years” on a website header would immediately build trust.

Nextdoor Presence — Neighborhood Trust

Being recommended on Nextdoor shows your neighbors trust you. This is a strong foundation — it just needs to be expanded beyond the neighborhood app to reach all of Robertsdale and surrounding areas.

Top 3 Things to Fix First

1. Claim Google Business Profile Immediately

Highest Priority

Go to business.google.com and create your listing. Select “Plumber” as your category, set your service area to Robertsdale, Silverhill, Loxley, Foley, and surrounding areas, and add your phone number. This is free and will put you on Google Maps within days. For a plumber, this single step will generate more calls than anything else.

2. Build a Simple Professional Website

Highest Priority

Create a clean, mobile-friendly website with your services (drain cleaning, pipe repair, water heaters, etc.), service area, phone number on every page, and a contact form. Include your 15+ year experience prominently. A plumbing website doesn’t need to be fancy — it needs to load fast, have your phone number visible, and tell Google what you do and where you do it.

3. Create a Facebook Business Page

Medium Priority

Set up a Facebook business page with your phone number, hours, and service area. When neighbors recommend you on Nextdoor or Facebook, having a business page gives people a place to learn more and call you directly. Ask your Nextdoor fans to leave a review on your Facebook page.
