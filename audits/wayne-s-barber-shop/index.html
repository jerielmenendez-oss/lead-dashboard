03. Prioritized Recommendations

  1. Build a professional, mobile-first website — A modern site with services, pricing, location map, hours, and online booking. This is the #1 priority and will immediately make the business discoverable.
  2. Optimize Google Business Profile — Add professional photos, complete service list, business description with keywords, and enable messaging. Post weekly updates.
  3. Set up Instagram and Facebook — Showcase haircuts, the shop atmosphere, and Gulf Shores lifestyle content. Visual content drives barber shop discovery.
  4. Implement local SEO strategy — Target keywords like "barber shop Gulf Shores," "men's haircut Gulf Shores AL," and "barber near me." Build citations on Yelp, Apple Maps, and Bing Places.

  6. Launch a review generation campaign — Actively ask satisfied customers to leave Google reviews. Respond to every review. Aim for 50+ reviews within 6 months.
