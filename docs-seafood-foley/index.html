Our Story

About Doc's

Doc's Seafood Shack & Oyster Bar has been serving up the freshest Gulf seafood in Foley, Alabama. From hand-shucked oysters to Southern-fried platters and sizzling steaks, we bring the coast to your plate.

Whether you're a local or just passing through on your way to the beach, Doc's is your home for honest, no-frills seafood done right.
