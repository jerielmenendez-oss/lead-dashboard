About Gulf Coast Auto Repair

Located on County Road 65 in Foley, Alabama, Gulf Coast Auto Repair is a locally owned and operated shop built on old-school values: honest work, fair pricing, and treating every customer like a neighbor.

Our ASE-trained mechanics bring decades of combined experience to every job, whether it's a quick oil change or a full engine overhaul. We work on all makes and models, foreign and domestic.

We don't upsell. We don't cut corners. We tell you what's wrong, what it'll cost, and we get it done right the first time.
