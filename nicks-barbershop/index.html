About Nick's

Founded by Nick, a U.S. military veteran, Nick's Barbershop has been a Foley staple for over 13 years. What started as one chair and a commitment to excellence has grown into a four-barber operation built on the values Nick learned in service: precision, discipline, and respect for every person who walks through the door.

No gimmicks. No pretense. Just clean cuts, straight razors, and the kind of attention to detail you'd expect from someone who spent years where details matter most.
