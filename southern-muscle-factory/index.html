ABOUT THE FACTORY

Southern Muscle Factory was founded by Marty Meredith, a proud United States Marine Corps veteran who believes a gym should be about one thing: the work.

Located in Foley, Alabama, just minutes from the Tanger Outlets, SMF packs 5,000 square feet of serious training equipment into a no-nonsense facility that never closes. Whether you're a local or just passing through the Gulf Coast, this is your gym.

Contact Amber or Marty to schedule a gym tour and see the factory floor for yourself.
