Our Story

A Hidden Gem on Highway 59

Tucked away in Stockton, Alabama, Stagecoach Cafe has been serving up the kind of home-cooked Southern meals that folks drive miles for. Owner Joyce Overstreet and her team plate up a rotating daily buffet of fried chicken, meatloaf, salmon patties, fresh vegetables, and cornbread that tastes like your grandmother made it.

Whether you're on your way to Fort Mims, passing through Baldwin County, or making a special trip just for the buttermilk pie, you'll leave full, happy, and already planning your next visit.

What We Serve

Daily Home-Style Buffet

🍗

Southern Fried Chicken

Golden, crispy, and seasoned just right. A Stagecoach staple served every single day alongside our rotating daily specials.

🥗

Homestyle Meatloaf

Thick-cut, glazed, and made from scratch. Paired with mashed potatoes and gravy that'll make you close your eyes and smile.

🍣

Salmon Patties

Pan-fried to a golden crust and served with tartar sauce. A Southern classic done the way it should be.

🥬

Fresh Vegetables

Turnip greens, fried okra, butter beans, mac & cheese, candied yams — choose your sides from the daily spread.

Buttermilk Pie

The legendary dessert. Creamy, custardy, perfectly sweet — top it with ice cream for a heavenly experience. You'll understand why it made the list.
